Rex

Rex Vistamp (レックスバイスタンプ, Rekkusu Baisutanpu): Serves as the personal Vistamp of Kamen Riders Revi and Vice, with the former using it in the Revice Driver to transform the two into their default form, Rex Genome. It also accidentally created the Rex Deadman when Hiromi Kadota attempted to use the Driver. Ikki can also stamp himself with the the Rex Vistamp to give Vice a physical body. It contains the genetic information of a Tyrannosaurus rex.
